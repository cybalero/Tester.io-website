The case.

Grade 5 aerospace titanium, CNC-milled from a single billet. Sapphire crystal, 2.8 mm thick, anti-reflective on both faces. Nothing glued — eight countersunk Torx-6 fixings, all replaceable.

Act II · Stack

The sensors.

Medical-grade ECG, SpO₂, skin-temp, and a nine-axis IMU — laid out on a flex PCB you can actually replace. Dual-band GPS antenna ringed around the bezel so it stays locked in a forest.

Act III · Core

The silicon.

An in-house M6 SoC running our open firmware — on-device speech, on-device health, zero data leaving the wrist. The battery flips out with one ribbon pull — no specialist required.

§ 11 — Answered

Questions, before you ask them.

The top eight we get in support each week. Can't find yours? We read every email within a business day — usually faster.

hello@tester.io
Does Tester ship outside the US?
Yes — to 38 countries, with duties pre-paid at checkout. Free shipping over $199 in the US, UK, EU, Canada, and Australia. Everywhere else, we use DHL Express with tracked delivery inside four business days.
What happens if something breaks?
Our repair program covers all parts for five years — batteries, displays, sensors, straps, bezels, the lot. Ship it to our Brooklyn or Rotterdam lab with the prepaid label in the box; most repairs return inside ten days. Small parts ship direct with instructions if you'd rather do it yourself.
Is there a monthly subscription?
No. Every feature on the spec sheet is included for the life of the device. Five years of software updates, unlimited cloud sync (if you opt in), voice dictation, and advanced health metrics — all paid for once, on the box.
Can I really run my own firmware?
Yes. The bootloader unlocks with a single command via USB-C. We maintain an open SDK on GitHub with a full hardware abstraction layer. Running custom firmware doesn't void your warranty on hardware — we just can't support your builds if they misbehave.
Which Matter and Thread devices does the hub support?
Anything with a Matter 1.3+ or Thread 1.3+ certification — we've tested 140+ devices across Aqara, Eve, Nanoleaf, Philips Hue, Yale, Shelly, and Ikea. Our hub also acts as a Thread border router, so it'll extend the mesh for any certified accessory on your network.
Do you track me with any of this?
No telemetry leaves your network by default. You can opt-in to anonymous crash reports if you want to help — it's a single toggle, disabled on first boot. Health and location data is encrypted at rest on the device and never transmitted unless you explicitly sync it to a service you control.
How does the 30-day trial work?
Buy anything, use it for up to 30 days, ship it back for a full refund. We pay return shipping. No restocking fee, no "unless the seal is broken" fine print — you can't evaluate a watch through a window.
Will my older Tester devices keep working?
Every device we've ever shipped still pairs with the current firmware. Our first-gen watch from 2022 got the same Matter update the Pro did last month. We maintain backwards compatibility for a minimum of seven years post-launch.
