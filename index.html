§ 02 — The Flow

Live in fifteen
minutes flat.

No onboarding calls. No consultants. Connect your repo, pick your gates, and start catching regressions before lunch.

01
Connect

Link your repository

Authorize GitHub, GitLab, or Bitbucket. Tester.io auto-detects your framework — Jest, Vitest, Cypress, Playwright, pytest — and wires up runners in seconds.

~ 30 seconds
02
Configure

Define your quality gates

Set thresholds per branch. Block merges on coverage drops, flake spikes, or critical failures. Or let the default policy run — it's good enough for most teams.

~ 4 minutes
